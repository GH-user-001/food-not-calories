# Today's Log Sheet Structure

This document outlines the structure of the "Today's Log" sheet in the "Calorie Tracker" spreadsheet.

## Columns

The sheet will have the following columns:

1.  **Time:**
    *   **Description:** Records the time the food item was consumed.
    *   **Data Type:** Time (e.g., HH:MM AM/PM or 24-hour format).
    *   **Example:** `08:30 AM` or `13:00`

2.  **Food Item:**
    *   **Description:** The name of the food or beverage consumed.
    *   **Data Type:** Text.
    *   **Example:** `Apple`, `Grilled Chicken Salad`

3.  **Calories:**
    *   **Description:** The number of calories for the specified food item and quantity.
    *   **Data Type:** Number.
    *   **Example:** `95`, `350`

4.  **Notes/Fun Fact:**
    *   **Description:** An optional field for any additional notes about the food, a related fun fact, or how you felt.
    *   **Data Type:** Text.
    *   **Example:** `Feeling energetic!`, `Apples are a good source of fiber.`

5.  **Meal Type:**
    *   **Description:** Categorizes the food item into a specific meal.
    *   **Data Type:** Text (Dropdown list).
    *   **Allowed Values:**
        *   Breakfast
        *   Lunch
        *   Dinner
        *   Snack
    *   **Example:** `Breakfast`

## Sheet Layout Example

| Time     | Food Item            | Calories | Notes/Fun Fact                     | Meal Type |
| :------- | :------------------- | :------- | :--------------------------------- | :-------- |
| 07:00 AM | Oatmeal with Berries | 300      | Good start to the day!             | Breakfast |
| 10:30 AM | Banana               | 105      | Potassium boost.                   | Snack     |
| 01:00 PM | Chicken Sandwich     | 450      | Used whole wheat bread.            | Lunch     |
| 04:00 PM | Almonds (handful)    | 160      | Healthy fats.                      | Snack     |
| 07:30 PM | Salmon with Quinoa   | 550      | Omega-3s for brain health!         | Dinner    |

This structure will help users effectively track their daily caloric intake and eating habits.
